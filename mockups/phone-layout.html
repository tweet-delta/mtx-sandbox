What the "after" is proposing

  • Fitting: question on its own line, Yes / No as two big side-by-side buttons — no more wrapping.
  • Fitting: Note becomes a small ✎ icon instead of a word, reclaiming width.
  • Fitting: tighter, phone-tuned header — house + date on one line, one control to switch house.
  • Context: periodic jobs (yearly, etc.) show a badge: last done + whether it's due now.
  • Context: a not-due job is dimmed and pre-satisfied, so it doesn't nag — but its every-visit companion check stays front and center.
